Seller Information and Guidelines

Welcome to the Seller Portal of the Blockchain Land Registry System. This guide explains how property owners can list lands and handle buyer requests securely.

Step 1: Listing Your Land

As a registered seller, you can list your land for sale by providing essential details such as location and ownership.

Land Location
Specify the exact location, including city and district.
Ownership Details
Your name and blockchain wallet address will be linked to the property for verification.
Land Status
Mark the land as “listed” to make it available for buyers to view.

Step 2: Managing Buyer Requests

When buyers submit a purchase request, you will be notified through your seller dashboard.

Review Requests
Each request contains the buyer’s details, message, and transaction intent.
Approve or Reject
You can approve one or multiple requests. Approved requests are recorded on-chain.
Land Transfer
Once approved, ownership details can be transferred after government verification.

Step 3: Completing the Sale

After final verification by the government officer, ownership records on the blockchain will update automatically.

Note: Every transaction is recorded permanently on the blockchain for legal transparency and dispute prevention.
