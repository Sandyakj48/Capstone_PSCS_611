KYC Verification Process

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification ensures every buyer, seller, and government officer interacting with the blockchain platform is authentic and legally validated.

Step 1: Submit Personal Identification

Aadhar / National ID
Upload a scanned copy of your government-issued ID card.
Photograph
A recent passport-sized photo is required for verification.
Signature and Thumb Impression
Provide digital copies for authenticity confirmation.

Step 2: Address & Citizenship Proof

Residential Address
Provide your complete postal address along with PIN code and region details.
Citizenship Proof
Ensure nationality matches with legal documentation to validate eligibility.

Step 3: Verification Outcome

Automatic Blockchain Recording
All KYC documents and verification timestamps are stored on the blockchain ledger.
Status Notification
Users receive an automated message once verification is approved by an officer.

Note: Once verified, KYC cannot be modified without re-verification. Blockchain ensures that your identity remains tamper-proof and secure.
