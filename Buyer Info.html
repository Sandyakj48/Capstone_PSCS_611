Buyer Information and Instructions

Welcome to the Buyer Portal of the Blockchain Based Land Registration System. This page guides you through the process of purchasing land securely and transparently using blockchain technology.

Step 1: Search for Land

You can search lands anywhere worldwide by specifying the desired area or location. The system will display verified properties owned by sellers with detailed information and blockchain-backed authenticity.

Step 2: Submit a Purchase Request

After selecting a property, you need to submit a formal purchase request to the seller. This request includes your personal details and your agreement to the terms and conditions.

Candidate Name
Your full name as it appears on official documents.
Father's Name
Used for identity verification.
Mother's Name
Used for identity verification.
Date of Birth
For legal verification and demographic details.
Mobile Number and Email
Essential for contact and verification purposes.
Nationality
Proof of citizenship required for property purchase.
Request Letter
A formal statement requesting approval from the seller, including agreeing to terms and conditions of the sale.

Note: This request is recorded on the blockchain to ensure transparency and immutability.

Step 3: Seller Approval & Extended Information

Once the seller approves your request, you will be asked to submit additional detailed information to comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Personal Details
This includes your full name, parents’ names, date of birth, contact information, and nationality again for verification.
Communication Address and Region Details
Your postal address, state, district, taluk, and pin code are used for official communication and records.
Socio-Economic Details
  • Caste and Income
  • Religion and Reservation Category
  • Caste Certificate and RD numbers
  • Mother Tongue
These details help in applying government policies and verifying eligibility for various benefits.
Payment Confirmation
Details about the fees paid for the land including date and method (e.g., via MetaMask).
Document Uploads
Upload your photo, Aadhar card, left-hand thumb impression, and signatures to comply with identity verification.
Legal Declaration
You must legally declare that the information provided is accurate and truthful, then provide a date and signature.

All submitted information and transactions are securely stored on the blockchain, ensuring your purchase is transparent, secure, and legally binding.
