Government Officer Information and Responsibilities

As a government officer, you are responsible for verifying and approving land transactions submitted on the blockchain-based land registry platform.

Step 1: Access Verification Requests

Each transaction pending approval will appear in your officer dashboard for review.

Verify Seller and Buyer Details
Ensure all submitted KYC and ownership documents match the blockchain records.
Land Validation
Cross-check the land ID, location, and registration documents before approval.

Step 2: Transaction Approval

Once verified, approve the request to finalize the transaction. The blockchain will automatically record your digital signature as part of the proof.

Rejection Option
If any mismatch or fraudulent activity is suspected, reject the request with remarks.
Transparency
All actions by officers are logged immutably on the blockchain to ensure accountability.

Note: Officers play a critical role in maintaining trust and legality within the blockchain registry system.
